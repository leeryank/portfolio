About

Ryan Lee


Born and raised in the heart of Silicon Valley , I've always been deeply immersed in the world of technology. With a background in video editing , animation , and graphic design , the visual aspects of tech fascinated me growing up.

Blending my creativity with a desire to understand the technical workings of software, I found a perfect match in UX/UI and product design . As a senior at the University of Miami    studying Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) , I've designed digital products for web, software, mobile, and virtual reality (VR), crafting pixel-perfect solutions to align business goals with human needs.

Beyond design, I'm a sports enthusiast , gym rat , music lover , and avid traveler . I thrive on exploring new places, immersing myself in different cultures, and drawing inspiration from the world around me.

I'm excited to collaborate with forward-thinking teams to shape the future of digital products and user experience. If you're looking for a passionate designer ready to make an impact, let's connect!
